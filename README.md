# Velora V2: The Creative Odyssey

Velora is a premium, AI-powered travel planning and experience engine designed to transform technical trip coordination into a soft, imaginative, and colorful narrative.

## 🌟 The Emotional Transformation

Velora V2 marks a complete paradigm shift from a technical, dark-mode "tool" to a warm, inviting, and creative exploration narrative. It is built to evoke the profound joy and wonder of travel through a curated, artisanal interface.

### Core Philosophy
- **Soft & Imaginative:** Moving away from hard-edged digital grids to organic curves and soft shadow play.
- **Premium Artisanal:** Every UI element feels handcrafted, like premium cardstock or a bespoke travel journal.
- **Narrative-Driven:** Travel isn't just about data; it's about the story. Velora architects curated expeditions that feel like a personal odyssey.

## 🎨 Visual Identity

- **Color Palette:** A warm, dusty palette of Bone, Rose, Terracotta, Sage Green, and Goldenrod, moving away from tech-blue gradients.
- **Typography:** 
    - **Display:** *Playfair Display* for elegant, prominent headings.
    - **UI:** *Inter* for clean, geometric but soft body text.
- **Art Direction:** Integrated graphics featuring watercolor washes, layered paper cutouts, and soft-focus aerial photography.

## 🚀 Key Features

- **Architectural AI Planning:** Uses advanced AI to generate bespoke itineraries across 195 countries.
- **Live Co-planning:** A social presence panel that shows friends exploring and planning in real-time with organic, premium user cards.
- **Global Passport:** A gamified collection of your travels, styled as a prestigious physical passport with commemorative stamps and XP.
- **Traveler DNA:** A personalized identity engine that lets you define your journey pace, interests, and budget profiles using a soft, tactile interface.
- **Interactive Compass:** A custom-built 3D Three.js visualization of a wireframe compass with light trails, replacing traditional abstract globes.

## 🛠️ Technical Craftsmanship

- **Frontend:** React 18+ with Vite
- **Animations:** Motion (Framer Motion) for eased, elastic transitions and drifting effects.
- **3D Elements:** Three.js for the central interactive compass.
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS with a custom-defined organic theme.
- **AI Core:** Integrated with Gemini APIs for intelligent itinerary generation.
